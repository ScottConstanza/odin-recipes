Mangu

Description

This is a signature breakfast dish that will have you full for a while. This paired with fried eggs, salami, and cheese make for an excellent way to wake up.

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salami
  • Fryable Cheese (Queso Tropical)
  • Butter

  • Optional - Red Onions

Steps

  1. Step 1: Peel the green plantains and cut them in 3 piece sections to add to a boiling pot.
  2. Step 2: As the plantains start to boil and soften up, add some salt for flavor.
  3. Step 3: Cut up some cheese and salami to fry up. Use desirable amount.
  4. Step 4: Heat up a skillet with some oil and start frying the cheese, then the salami. Once both are done, fry your eggs.
  5. Step 5: Take out the boiled plantains and put them into a bowl. Add some butter and mash it together while adding some of the water you used to boil it along with some cold water.
  6. Step 6: Once mashed up to a mashed potatoe texture, add the other ingredients to enjoy!

  7. Bonus Step - Cut up some red onions and sautee them in the same skillet you used with the oil for extra flavor on your dish.
