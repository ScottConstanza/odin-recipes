Cafe con Leche

Description

This simple dish could make a great start to your day. Brew up some coffee, add some milk, and have a nice buttered toasted bread/bagel to dip in the cup. Now that's priceless and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Bread or Bagel
  • Butter
  • Any type of milk
  • Coffee

Steps

  1. Step 1 - Put some coffee to brew.
  2. Step 2 - While the coffee brews, start toasting up your bread or bagel.
  3. Step 3 - As the coffee starts to rise/brew, add some butter to your bread for a nice soft flavor.
  4. Step 4 - Add coffee to a cup and put a desirable amount of sugar. Then add some milk.
  5. Step 5 - Enjoy the quick and easy breakfast. You can dip in the bread for added flavor or you can drink it after a bite of the toast. Enjoy!
