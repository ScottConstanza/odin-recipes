Pan con Queso

Description

Just some bread and fried cheese or any type of cheese you'd like. This simple little lunch can be combined with fresh OJ or passion fruit juice. Be aware, you may become addicted to this.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Step 1 - Toast up the bread.
  2. Step 2 - Cut up some fried cheese and fry it up on a oiled skillet. Or you can just melt a different type of cheese while you toast the bread.
  3. Step 3 - Add butter to one side of the bread after it's warm from toasting.
  4. Step 4 - Enjoy with a cup of juice or coffee!
